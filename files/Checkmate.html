A checkmate occurs when a king is placed in check and has no legal moves to escape.

Basic Checkmates

In order to checkmate the enemy king with limited pieces, you will generally need to force the enemy king to the corner of the chessboard. You will need to use your king actively to force checkmate. The easiest way to accomplish this is to visualize the opposing king in a box. Every move you make should shrink the box, thereby, limiting the number of squares the enemy king can use. Shrinking the box will eventually force the opposing king into a corner and will lead to checkmate.
